The Problem

Enterprise SaaS monitoring is broken.

The monitoring tools that exist either report their own performance, run scripted synthetic tests that miss the real picture, or cost so much only the largest enterprises can justify them. None of them tell you what your users are actually experiencing.

Vendor status pages

The fox is guarding the henhouse. Vendors report their own uptime — on infrastructure they control, using metrics they define. You have no way to independently verify their claims.

Synthetic tests miss the point

Enterprise DEM tools run scripted tests from controlled machines. They tell you your script ran — not what your users experienced. Real slowdowns slip through because no synthetic test can replicate every workflow, every region, every real session.

Gut feeling & complaints

IT teams rely on user complaints to know when Jira is slow. By the time the help desk tickets pile up, your team has lost hours of productivity. That's not a monitoring strategy.

How It Works

Independent monitoring in 3 steps.

01

Install

Add the Chrome extension in seconds. Zero configuration required for end users. Deploy org-wide via Chrome MDM policy or simply share a link with your team.

02

Measure

The extension passively intercepts and times real API calls from your SaaS tools as users work normally. No synthetic tests, no altered behavior — just accurate real-world data from genuine sessions.

03

Benchmark

Compare your SaaS performance against the global median across thousands of organizations. Export SLA compliance reports with hard data for vendor negotiations and renewal conversations.
